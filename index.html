About the Entrepreneurship Club

The Adamas University Entrepreneurship Club is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering innovation, leadership, and entrepreneurial thinking among students. We provide a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to develop their ideas, learn essential business skills, and connect with like-minded peers and industry mentors.

Through our diverse range of programs, workshops, and networking events, we empower students to turn their entrepreneurial dreams into successful ventures. Whether you're a budding startup founder or simply curious about the world of business, our club offers the resources and support you need to thrive in today's dynamic startup ecosystem.

GES'26 - Global Entrepreneurship Summit

The Global Entrepreneurship Summit (GES) is IIT Kharagpur's annual international corporate summit, organized by the Entrepreneurship Cell. Since its inception in 2007, GES has become India's largest student-run entrepreneurial summit, attracting visionaries from across industries.
