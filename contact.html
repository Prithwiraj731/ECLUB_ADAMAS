Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about joining and participating in the club

How do I join the Entrepreneurship Club?

Simply fill out the contact form above expressing your interest, or attend one of our open meetings held at the beginning of each semester. Membership is open to all Adamas University students.

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership is completely free! We believe in making entrepreneurship accessible to all students who have the passion and drive to innovate.

Do I need prior business experience?

Not at all! Our club welcomes students from all backgrounds and experience levels. We provide training and resources to help you develop your entrepreneurial skills from scratch.

What kind of events do you organize?

We host a variety of events including workshops, guest speaker sessions, hackathons, pitch competitions, networking meetups, and our flagship Startup Bootcamp. Check our Events section for upcoming programs.

Can I get help developing my startup idea?

Absolutely! We offer mentorship programs, incubation support, and resources to help you develop and launch your startup. Our network includes experienced entrepreneurs and industry experts ready to guide you.

How can I stay updated about club activities?

Follow us on our social media channels and subscribe to our newsletter by contacting us. We regularly post updates about upcoming events, opportunities, and club news.
